Meet the Fellows

LaCreshia

Community Organizer & Cooperative Developer

Organizer of the Chicago Juneteenth campaign and cooperative developer with Upside Down Consulting, supporting formerly incarcerated worker-owners. Leads citywide reparation efforts and grassroots organizing for Black cultural memory.

Major Purnell

Entrepreneur & Faith-Based Anchor

Business consultant helping over 500 entrepreneurs through The Hatchery and more. Envisions a West Side family center with shared ownership, recreation, and youth engagement. Aims to become a long-term economic anchor.

Mercedes Pickett

Technologist & Youth Organizer

Founder of Earth Remedies and ER Data Solutions. Leads a youth-powered database mapping ecosystem resources and needs. Advocates for community ownership of data and decentralized tech infrastructure rooted in equity and Black futures.
